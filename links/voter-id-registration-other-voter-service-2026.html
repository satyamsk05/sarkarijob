The Voter ID, officially called the Election Photo Identity Card (EPIC), is a photo identification document issued by the Election Commission of India (ECI) to Indian citizens who are eligible to vote. Its main purpose is to verify a voter’s identity, enhance the efficiency of the voting process, and help prevent impersonation and electoral fraud during democratic elections. It is also popularly referred to as an election card, voter’s card, or simply a Voter ID card.

 Structure of Voter ID 
In India, a voter ID card serves as a valid form of personal identification since it is issued by an official government authority. It includes the following information.
  • A unique Serial number (EPIC Number)
  • Photograph of the cardholder
  • A hologram containing the respective state/national symbol
  • Name of cardholder
  • Father’s Name of cardholder
  • Gender of cardholder
  • Card holder’s Date of Birth
 
 Voter ID Card Eligibility 
To qualify for a Voter ID card, individuals must satisfy the following requirements.
  • An Indian citizen
  • Must be aged 18 years or above
  • Must have a permanent address
 
 Documents Required For Voter ID Card 
Applying for a Voters ID requires you to submit the following documents.
  • Passport size color Photograph
  • Identity Proof
  • Address Proof
 
 How to Apply for Voter ID Card Online 
  • Step 1: Go to the official National Voters' Services Portal (NVSP) website.
  • Step 2: On the homepage, locate and click the ‘Sign-Up’ button at the top right corner.
  • Step 3: Provide your mobile number, email address, and the captcha code, then click ‘Continue’.
  • Step 4: Fill in your ‘First Name’, ‘Last Name’, set a ‘Password’, confirm it, and press ‘Request OTP’.
  • Step 5: Enter the OTP received on your mobile number and email, then click ‘Verify’.
  • Step 6: After verification, click on ‘Login’. Enter your mobile number, password, captcha code, and then click ‘Request OTP’.
  • Step 7: Input the OTP sent to your mobile device and click the ‘Verify & Login’ button.
  • Step 8: Once logged in, select the ‘Fill Form 6’ option under the ‘New registration for general electors’ section.
  • Step 9: Complete Form 6 by entering your personal information, details of relatives, contact information, Aadhaar number, date of birth, address, and a declaration. Upload all necessary documents in the designated sections, then click ‘Preview and Submit’.
  • Step 10: Review all the entered information carefully. If everything is correct, click on ‘Submit’.
 
 How to Apply for Voter ID Card Offline 
  • Step 1: Visit the nearest State Election Office and request Form 6.
  • Step 2: Fill in the Form 6 with the required details.
  • Step 3: Submit the completed Form 6 along with a photo and supporting documents at the Election Office.
