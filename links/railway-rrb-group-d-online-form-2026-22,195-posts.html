 RRB Group D Various Posts in Level 1 2026 While Form Filling Document Required 
Documents Name Details
Photo & Signature
  • The candidate’s photograph must be a recent passport-size photo with a light or white background. The face should be clearly visible, and the candidate must not wear a cap, goggles, or mask.
  • The signature must be done using black ink on white paper and should be clear and easily readable.
Aadhaar Card
  • The Aadhaar number is required at the time of form filling, and the original Aadhaar card must be produced during PET and document verification.
10th Class Marksheet
  • This document is used as proof of Date of Birth and educational qualification.
Caste / Category Certificates (If Applicable)
  • Candidates must possess a valid SC/ST certificate, OBC (Non-Creamy Layer) certificate, or EWS certificate of the latest financial year, as applicable. The certificate must be issued in the government-prescribed format.
PwBD / Disability Certificate (If Applicable)
  • Candidates must have a Benchmark Disability Certificate issued by an authorized medical board, with the disability percentage clearly mentioned.
Ex-Serviceman Certificate (If Applicable)
  • Candidates must possess a valid Discharge Book or Service Certificate, in which the length of service is clearly mentioned.
Bank Account Details
  • Candidates must provide the account holder’s name, account number, and IFSC code, which are required for fee refund or future payments.
Domicile / Address Proof (If Asked)
  • Candidates may need to provide a state domicile certificate along with their permanent and correspondence address details, if required.
Other Useful Documents
  • Candidates must keep an active email ID and mobile number, along with the application/confirmation page printout and fee payment receipt, for future reference.
  • Candidates must Keep all documents scanned and ready before form filling. Fill name, DOB, and parents’ names exactly as per 10th marksheet. Incorrect or fake documents can cancel selection. Carry originals with self-attested photocopies for verification.
