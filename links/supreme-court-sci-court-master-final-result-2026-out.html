 Supreme Court SCI Court Master Recruitment 2025 : Education Qualification 
  • Candidates must have a Law degree, shorthand speed of 120 w.p.m. in English, and computer typing speed of 40 w.p.m. They should also have at least 5 years of regular experience as a Private Secretary, PA, or Stenographer in a government department, PSU, or statutory body.

 How To Check & Download Supreme Court SCI Court Master Final Result 2026 
  • Visit the official website of the Supreme Court of India – https://main.sci.gov.in
  • Click on the link for “Court Master Result 2026.”
  • Enter your Application Number, Date of Birth, or Registration ID.
  • Click on Submit.
  • Your result will appear on the screen.
  • Download and take a printout for future reference.
