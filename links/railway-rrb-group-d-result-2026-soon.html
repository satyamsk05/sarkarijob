 How To Check & Download Railway RRB Group D Result 2026 
  • Open your internet browser on your mobile phone or computer.
  • Go to the official website of the Railway Recruitment Board.
  • On the homepage, find the “Results” or “Latest Notifications” section.
  • Look for the link titled “RRB Group D Result 2026”.
  • Click on the result notification link.
  • The result PDF will open on the screen.
  • Press Ctrl + F on your keyboard (or use the search option on mobile).
  • Enter your Roll Number or Registration Number to find your result.
  • If your roll number appears in the list, you have qualified for the next stage.
  • Download the result PDF and take a printout for future reference.

 Railway RRB Group D Result 2026 : Important Question 
  • Question: When will the Railway RRB Group D Result 2026 be declared?
  • Answer: The result is usually released a few weeks after the completion of the Computer Based Test (CBT) on the official RRB regional websites.
  • Question: How can candidates check the RRB Group D Result 2026?
  • Answer: Candidates can check the result by visiting the official RRB website and opening the RRB Group D Result 2026 PDF in the results section.
  • Question: What details are required to check the result?
  • Answer: Candidates usually need their Roll Number or Registration Number to search their result in the PDF list.
  • Question: What happens after the RRB Group D CBT result?
  • Answer: Candidates who qualify the CBT will be called for the Physical Efficiency Test (PET) followed by Document Verification and Medical Examination.
  • Question: What is the official website for RRB?
  • Answer: The official website for RRB is https://indianrailways.gov.in/
