Post Name Eligibility Criteria
Principal
  • Candidate must hold a Master’s Degree in any subject along with a B.Ed qualification and should also possess a minimum of 12 years of teaching experience.
TGT
  • Candidate must have a Bachelor’s Degree in the relevant subject with at least 50% marks, along with a B.Ed qualification. In addition, the candidate should have passed the STET or CTET Paper II examination.
PGT
  • Candidate must possess a Master’s Degree in the relevant subject with at least 50% marks along with a B.Ed. degree. However, for those who have completed an integrated 4-year degree course, a separate B.Ed. qualification is not required. For subject-wise vacancy details, candidates should refer to the official notification.
Staff Nurse (Female)
  • Candidate must have completed B.Sc. Nursing or Post Basic Nursing and be registered with the State Nursing Council. In addition, a minimum of 2.5 years of relevant work experience is required.
Hostel Warden
  • Candidate must hold a Bachelor’s Degree in any stream from a recognized university in India.
Accountant
  • Candidate must have a Bachelor’s Degree in Commerce (B.Com) from any recognized university in India.
Junior Secretariat Assistant (JSA)
  • Candidate must have passed the 10+2 Intermediate Examination and should possess a typing speed of 35 words per minute in English or 30 words per minute in Hindi.
Lab Attendant
  • Candidate must have passed Class 10 with a Certificate/Diploma in Laboratory Technique or must have passed 10+2 Intermediate with Science stream.
