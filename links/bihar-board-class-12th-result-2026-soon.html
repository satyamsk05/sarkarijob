 How To Check & Download Bihar Board BSEB Class 12th Inter Result 2026 
  • Open your internet browser on your mobile or computer.
  • Go to the official website of the Bihar School Examination Board.
  • On the homepage, find the section “Intermediate Result 2026”.
  • Click on the link titled “BSEB Class 12th / Inter Result 2026”.
  • Enter your Roll Number and Roll Code (mentioned on your admit card).
  • Fill the captcha code if required.
  • Click on the View / Submit button.
  • Your Bihar Board 12th result 2026 will appear on the screen.
  • Download the scorecard PDF and save it on your device.
  • Take a printout for future admission or reference.
