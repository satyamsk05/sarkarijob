UP Police Constable 32,679 Post Re-Exam Date 2026

UPPRPB Uttar Pradesh Police Constable Civil Police Recruitment 2025-26

Exam Dates: 08, 09, 10 June 2026 Vacancy: 32,679 Posts
Latest Exam Update

The Uttar Pradesh Police Recruitment and Promotion Board (UPPRPB) has announced the Written Exam Dates for the 32,679 Constable recruitment. The examination will be conducted in multiple shifts on June 8, 9, and 10, 2026. Candidates are advised to prepare for the OMR-based written test as per the latest syllabus.
