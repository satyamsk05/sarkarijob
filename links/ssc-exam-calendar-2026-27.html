 How To Check & Download The SSC Exam Calendar 2026-2027 

Steps to Check and Download the SSC Calendar 2026:

    1. Visit the Official SSC Website:
        • Go to the official SSC website: ssc.nic.in.
    1. Navigate to the Latest News Section:
        • On the homepage, look for the “Latest News” or “Notices” section. The calendar is usually announced here.
    1. Check for SSC Calendar Notification:
        • Look for a notification titled “SSC Annual Calendar 2026” .
    1. Download the PDF:
        • Click on the notification link. It will open a PDF document with the detailed schedule for various SSC exams in 2025.
        • Click on the download icon or right-click and select “Save As” to download the calendar to your device.
    1. View Exam Dates:
        • Open the downloaded PDF to check the tentative dates for exams like SSC CGL, CHSL, MTS, GD Constable, and others.
