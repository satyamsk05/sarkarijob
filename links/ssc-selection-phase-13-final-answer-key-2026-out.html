 How To Check & Download SSC Phase 13 Result 2026 
  • Visit the official SSC website.
  • On the homepage, find and click on the “Answer Key” section.
  • Look for the notification titled “Selection Post Phase-13 Result 2026”.
  • Click on the provided link to access the login page.
  • Enter your Registration Number / Roll Number and Password / Date of Birth.
  • Submit the details to view your Result and Response Sheet.
  • Download the answer key PDF and save it to your device.
  • Match your responses with the official key to calculate your estimated score.
  • If you find any discrepancies, you can submit objections within the given time frame by following the instructions provided in the notification.
  • After the objection window closes, SSC will release the result, which will be used for result preparation.
