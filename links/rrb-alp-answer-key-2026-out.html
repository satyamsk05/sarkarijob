 How To Check & Download Railway RRB ALP Answer Key 2026 
  • Open the official Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) website of your region.
  • Go to the CEN Notification / Answer Key section.
  • Find and click on RRB ALP Answer Key 2026.
  • You will be redirected to the candidate login page.
  • Enter your Registration Number.
  • Enter your Date of Birth in the required format.
  • Fill in the captcha/security code shown on the screen.
  • Click on the Login / Submit button.
  • Your Answer Key will be displayed on the screen.
  • Download or take a printout of the Answer Key for reference.
