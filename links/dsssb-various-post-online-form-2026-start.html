 DSSSB Various Post Recruitment 2026 : Education Qualification 
Post Name Eligibility 
Assistant Manager (Tourism)
  • Candidates must possess a Master’s Degree in Tourism or a Postgraduate Diploma in Tourism or a Postgraduate Diploma in Travel & Tourism.
Assistant Manager (General)
  • Candidates must possess a Master’s in Business Administration (MBA) in any discipline.
Assistant Manager (Accounts)
  • Candidates must be a Chartered Accountant (CA) or Cost and Management Accountant (CMA) from a recognized university with at least 3 years of experience in Accounts and Finance.
Assistant Manager (Legal)
  • Candidates must possess an LLB degree from a recognized university with at least 3 years of experience in the relevant field.
Assistant Manager (Air Ticketing)
  • Candidates must be a graduate with IATA/UFTAA diploma or a Tourism graduate with IATA-approved certificate in Ticketing/Fare Construction, having 5 years’ experience in international air ticketing, including 4 years with an IATA-approved agent.
Assistant Manager (Public Relation)
  • Candidates must be a graduate with a Diploma in PR, Advertising, Mass Communication, or Journalism and have at least 2 years of relevant experience.
Assistant Grade – I (IT)
  • Candidates must possess a B.Tech. degree in Computer Engineering, Computer Science, Computer Technology, or Information Technology from a recognized university.
Radiographer
  • Candidates must have Sr. Secondary (10+2) with Science from a recognized board or university, along with either a 2-year Certificate or 2-year Diploma in Radiography, or a B.Sc. in Radiography or Radiological Technology (2 years) from a recognized institute.
Optometrist
  • Candidates must have Class XII (Science) from a recognized board and a four-year Degree in Optometry from a recognized university.
  • Note: Qualifications may be relaxed at the discretion of the competent authority for otherwise well-qualified candidates.
Junior Radiographer
  • Candidates must have Matriculation (10th pass) with at least 2 years of experience in an X-Ray Dark Room.
Meter & Relay Tester Grade - II
  • Candidates must be Matriculate and possess either an ITI qualification in the Electrician trade or Competency Class – I certification.
Reporter
  • Candidates must have a Bachelor’s degree from a recognized university and pass the skill test: dictation for 10 minutes at 100 WPM (English/Hindi) and computer transcription for 40 minutes (English) / 55 minutes (Hindi). Qualifications may be relaxed by the competent authority for well-qualified candidates.
Katib (Calligrapher)
  • Candidates must have Senior Secondary (10+2) or equivalent with Urdu as an elective from a recognized board, and a 2-year course in Khattati (Calligraphy) from a recognized institute.
Patwari
  • Candidates must be a graduate from any recognized university or possess an equivalent qualification, with proficiency in computers and working knowledge of Urdu or Hindi.
Junior Law Officer
  • Candidates must possess a regular Degree in Law (entitling them for Bar registration and court practice) from a recognized university or equivalent, along with 5 years of experience at the Bar.
Chemical Assistant
  • Candidates must have a B.Sc. with Chemistry as one of the subjects from a recognized university, along with 2 years’ experience in Chemical Analysis, Research, or intoxicating substance analysis from a Government laboratory or NABL-approved private institute, in roles such as Analyst, Jr. Analyst, Chemical Assistant, Chemist, Scientist, or Assistant Scientist.
Stenographer Grade-I
  • Candidates must possess a Bachelor’s Degree from a recognized university or institution, have passed English Shorthand and Typing (120 wpm & 40 wpm on computer) or Hindi Shorthand and Typing (120 wpm & 35 wpm on computer), and possess at least 5 years of experience as a Stenographer in a Government, Public Sector, Autonomous body, or reputed Private firm.
Junior Laboratory Assistant
  • Candidates must have either Class XII (Science stream) from a recognized board with 3 years’ laboratory experience or a Degree in Science from a recognized university/institution.
Book Binder
  • Candidates must possess an ITI in Book Binding or an equivalent qualification from a recognized board or institution.
Assistant Microphotographist
  • Candidates must have a B.Sc. with Physics as a subject from a recognized university and a certificate course in Reprography from the National Archives of India.
