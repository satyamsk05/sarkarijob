  • Candidates must have a Bachelor’s degree in any discipline from a recognized Indian university and must have passed at least 6 papers of the examination conducted by the Institute of Actuaries of India or the Institute and Faculty of Actuaries, UK. AAO (Insurance Specialist) 310
  • Candidates must have a Bachelor’s degree in any discipline along with a professional qualification in Life Insurance (Fellowship of Insurance Institute of India). They should also have at least 5 years of experience working in IRDAI-regulated Life Insurance Companies. The age limit is 21 to 30 years, i.e., candidates must be born between 02 August 1995 and 01 August 2004 (both dates inclusive).
AAO (Legal) 30
  • Candidates must have a Bachelor’s degree in Law with at least 50% marks (45% for SC/ST and PwBD). They should be enrolled with the Bar Council as an Advocate and must have at least 2 years of experience either as an Advocate or as a Law Officer in the Legal Department of a bank, financial institution, statutory corporation, company, or State/Central Government. The age limit is 21 to 32 years, i.e., candidates must be born between 02 August 1993 and 01 August 2004 (both dates inclusive).

 How To Check & Download LIC AAO / AE Interview Letter 2026 
  • Open your internet browser on your mobile phone or computer.
  • Go to the official website of the Life Insurance Corporation of India.
  • On the homepage, find and click on the “Careers” section.
  • Look for the link titled “LIC AAO / AE Interview Letter 2026”.
  • Click on the interview call letter download link.
  • Enter your Registration Number / Roll Number and Password or Date of Birth.
  • Fill in the captcha code if required.
  • Click on the Login / Submit button.
  • Your LIC AAO / AE Interview Letter 2026 will appear on the screen.
  • Download the PDF and take a printout for the interview.
