 How To Check & Download MPESB Police SI and Subedar Result 2026 
  • Visit the official portal of the Madhya Pradesh Employees Selection Board (MPESB).
  • On the homepage, go to the “Results / Recruitment Results” section.
  • Look for the link labeled “MPESB Police SI and Subedar Result 2026” or “Police Sub Inspector & Subedar Scorecard / Merit List.”
  • Click on the result link when it becomes active (after the result is announced).
  • Enter your Application Number / Registration Number.
  • Enter your Date of Birth (and other required details if asked, like mother’s name initials).
  • Click Submit / Login to view your result status.
  • Your result / scorecard will be displayed on the screen.
  • Download and save the PDF or scorecard to your device.
  • Take a printout for future reference and for the next stage of the selection process.
