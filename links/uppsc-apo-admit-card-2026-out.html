 How To Check & Download UPPSC APO Admit Card 2026 
  • Open your internet browser on your mobile phone or computer.
  • Go to the official website of the Uttar Pradesh Public Service Commission.
  • On the homepage, go to the “Candidate’s Corner” or “Download Admit Card” section.
  • Look for the link titled “UPPSC APO Admit Card 2026”.
  • Click on the admit card download link.
  • Enter your OTR Number / Registration Number, Date of Birth, and other required details.
  • Fill in the captcha code if required.
  • Click on the Submit / Download Admit Card button.
  • Your UPPSC APO Admit Card 2026 will appear on the screen.
  • Download the admit card and take a clear printout for the examination.
