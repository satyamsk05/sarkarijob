UP Scholarship Online Form 2025-26

Post Date: January 5, 2026

2:21 pm

Social Welfare Department of Uttar Pradesh, has released the notification for the UP Scholarship 2025-26 for students of Class 9–10 (Pre-Matric), Class 11–12 (Post-Matric), and Post-Matric Other Than Intermediate. The online application process will be open from 02 July 2025 to 30 October 2025 for Pre-Matric and Post-Matric, and from 10 July 2025 to 20 December 2025 for Post-Matric Other Than Intermediate. Interested candidates must check the eligibility criteria, age limit, and read the full notification before applying. Eligible students can submit their applications online within the given dates.

UP Scholarship Online Form 2025-26 : Examination Details
 UP Scholarship Online Form 2025-26 : Document Required 
  • Candidates have to attach the following documents along with the Hard copy of the Online Application Form:
  • Last Qualifying Exam Mark Sheet
  • Caste Certificate
  • Income Certificate
  • Bank Passbook
  • Fee Receipt Number
  • Annual Non Refundable Amount
  • Enrollment Number
  • Aadhar Card Number
  • Latest Passport Size Scan Photo
  • For Renewal Candidates – Kindly Use Last Year Registration Number to Login to Renewal Section and Enter the Fresh Details
 
Scholarship Name For Class
Pre Matric Class 09th & Class 10th
Post Matric Class 11th & Class 12th
Dashmottar UG / PG / Diploma & Certificate Courses
 
Criteria Eligibility
Domicile
  • Candidates hailing from Uttar Pradesh and having Domicile of Uttar Pradesh can only avail this Scholarship facility.
Pre Matric
  • For Class 09th candidates they should clear their class 8th level of examination and enrolled with Class 09th & For Class 10th candidates they should clear their class 09th level of examination and enrolled with Class 10th can fill this Scholarship Form.
Post Matric
  • For Class 11th candidates they should clear their class 10th level of examination and enrolled with Class 11h & For Class 12th candidates they should clear their class 11th level of examination and enrolled with Class 12th can fill this Scholarship Form.
Dashmottar
  • Candidates must be enrolled in any UG / PG / Diploma / Certificate Examination can fill up this scholarship form.

 How To Fill UP Scholarship Online Form 2025-26 
  • Interested candidates who wish to apply for the UP Scholarship post can submit their application online before 20 December 2025.
  • Use the click here link provided below under important link section to apply directly.
  • Alternatively, visit the official website of UP Scholarship to complete the application process online.
  • Make sure to complete the application before the deadline  20 December 2025.
 
 UP Scholarship Form For Offline Submission 2025 
  • After filling the Online Scholarship Form candidates need to take the print put of complete form and after that they have to attach relevant documents (specified above) & are required to send it to the respective College / School / Institution before due Date.
