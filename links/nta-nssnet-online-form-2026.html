Exam Name Education Qualification
Class 6th (VI) 
  • Candidates must be between 10 and 12 years of age as on 31 March 2026. The applicant should be born between 01 April 2014 and 31 March 2016. Students must secure at least 65% marks (78 marks) overall in the NSSNET 2026 examination and also obtain a minimum of 50% marks in each subject to qualify.
Class 7th (VII)
  • Candidates must be between 11 and 13 years of age as on 31 March 2026. The applicant should be born between 01 April 2013 and 31 March 2015. Students must secure at least 65% marks (78 marks) overall in the NSSNET 2026 examination and also obtain a minimum of 50% marks in each subject.
