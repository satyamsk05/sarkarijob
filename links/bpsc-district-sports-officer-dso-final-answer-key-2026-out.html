BPSC District Sports Officer DSO
  • Candidates must be graduates from a recognized university with a Diploma/PG Diploma in Sports Coaching from SAI, LNIPE Gwalior, a Central Sports University, or any UGC/Bihar-recognized sports university. They should also have sports achievements such as representing India in international events (Olympics, Commonwealth, Asian Games), representing the state at national level, or multiple participations in recognized university, youth, inter-services, police, or railway championships.
