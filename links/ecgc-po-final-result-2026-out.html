 How To Check & Download ECGC Probationary Officer PO Final Result 2026 
  • Open the official Export Credit Guarantee Corporation (ECGC) website.
  • Go to the Careers / Recruitment / Final Results section.
  • Find and click on ECGC Probationary Officer (PO) Final Result 2026.
  • The result notice will open in PDF format or redirect to a login page.
  • If a PDF opens, search your Roll Number / Registration Number in the list.
  • If login is required, enter your Registration Number and Date of Birth / Password.
  • Click on the Submit / View Result option.
  • Your qualifying status will be displayed on the screen.
  • Download the final result PDF / result details and save it.
  • Take a printout for future reference.
