 How To Check & Download The BPSC Exam Calendar 2026 

  • Visit the Official BPSC Website:
    Go to bpsc.gov.in.

  • Go to the 'Examinations' Section:
    On the homepage, hover over the “Examinations” tab in the top menu.

  • Select 'Calendar':
    From the dropdown menu, click on “Calendar”.

  • Access the Annual Calendar 2026:
    On the Calendar page, find and click on the link titled “Annual Calendar 2026”.

  • Download the PDF:
    The BPSC Exam Calendar 2026 PDF will open. You can download or print it for your reference.
