NTA NIFTEE Online Form 2026 : Vacancy Details
Course Name Eligibility Criteria
Bachelor of Design (B.Des)
  • Candidates must have passed Class 12th from any recognized board, including NIOS, or must hold a 3–4 year diploma or an equivalent qualification approved by a recognized authority. Applicants must also meet the prescribed age limit, follow the official guidelines, and be Indian citizens to apply.
Bachelor of Fashion Technology (B.F.Tech.)
  • Candidates must have passed Class 12th with Mathematics as a subject, or they must hold a 3–4 year Engineering Diploma from a recognized institution. Applicants must also fulfill the required age criteria and must be citizens of India to be considered eligible.
Master of Design (M. Des.)
  • Candidates must have a Bachelor’s Degree in any stream from a recognized university, or they must possess a 3-year Diploma from NIFT or NID. Applicants should also meet the prescribed age limit and must be citizens of India to qualify for the application process.
Master of Fashion Management (M.F.M.)
  • Candidates must have a Bachelor’s Degree in any stream from a recognized university, or a 3-year Diploma from NIFT or NID to be eligible. Along with the educational qualification, applicants must also fulfill the required age criteria and be Indian citizens as per official guidelines.
Master of Fashion Technology (M.F.Tech.)
  • Candidates must have completed B.F.Tech from NIFT or hold a B.E./B.Tech degree from a recognized university or institution. Applicants must also satisfy the age requirements and be citizens of India as per official rules.
Bachelor of Design (B.Des.) For Artisan Category
  • Candidates must have passed Class 12th from a recognized board, including NIOS, or possess a 3–4 year Diploma or an equivalent qualification. Additionally, applicants must have a valid Artisan Card. Along with the educational criteria, candidates must also fulfill the prescribed age limit and be citizens of India as per official guidelines.

 How To Check & Download NTA NIFTEE Stage-I Result 2026 
  • Visit the official website of the National Testing Agency (NTA).
  • On the homepage, go to the Examination / Results / Answer Key section.
  • Find the notification or link that says “NIFTEE 2026 Stage-I Result.”
  • Click on the Answer Key link when it becomes active.
  • You will be asked to log in using your Application Number / Registration Number and Date of Birth or Password.
  • After logging in, your provisional result will be displayed on the screen.
  • Download and save the result PDF for future reference.
 
 NTA NIFTEE Online Form 2026  : Mode Of Selection 

  • UG Programmes : For B.Des, candidates must appear in GAT, CAT, and Situation Test. The weightage is CAT 50%, GAT 30%, and Situation Test 20%. For B.F.Tech, candidates only need to appear in GAT.

  • PG Programmes : For M.Des, candidates must appear in GAT, CAT, and a Personal Interview. For MFM and M.F.Tech, candidates must appear in GAT and a Personal Interview.

  • Marking Scheme : Each correct answer earns 1 mark, each wrong answer deducts 0.25 marks, and no marks are awarded for unattempted questions.
