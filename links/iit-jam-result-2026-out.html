 How To Check & Download IIT Delhi JAM 2026 Exam Result 
  • Visit the official IIT JAM 2026 website
  • Click on JOAPS (JAM Online Application Processing System)
  • Select the option “Result”
  • Enter your Enrollment ID or Email ID
  • Enter your Password
  • Fill in the captcha code (if displayed)

  • Your IIT JAM 2026 Result will appear on the screen
  • Click on Download Result
