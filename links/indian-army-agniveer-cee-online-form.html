Vacancy Details & Eligibility

Post Category Eligibility Criteria
Agniveer General Duty (GD) Class 10th Matric with 45% marks in aggregate and 33% in each subject.
Agniveer Technical 10+2 Intermediate in Science (Physics, Chemistry, Maths, English) with 50% marks OR ITI Course (1 Year).
Agniveer Office Asst / Clerk 10+2 Intermediate in Any Stream with 60% marks and 50% in English & Maths.
Agniveer Tradesman (10th) Class 10th High School Exam Passed. Minimum 33% in each subject.
Agniveer Tradesman (8th) Class 8th Exam Passed. Minimum 33% in each subject.
Sepoy Pharma 10+2 Qualified and D.Pharma with 55% Marks OR B.Pharma with 50% Marks.
Women Military Police Class 10th Matric with 45% marks and 33% in each subject. Same as GD.
