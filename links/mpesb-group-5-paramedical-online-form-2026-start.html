Physiotherapist
  • Bachelor’s degree in Physical Therapy (BPT) with registration under the MP Paramedical Council is required. Pay Scale: ₹36,200 – ₹1,14,800 (Level-9). For full details, refer to the official notification.
Counselor
  • Master’s degree in Social Work (MSW) with a Post Graduate Diploma in Counseling and Family Therapy (PGDCFT) is required. Pay Scale: ₹25,300 – ₹80,500 (Level-6).
Pharmacist Grade II
  • 10+2 (Intermediate) with PCB subjects, along with a Degree or Diploma in Pharmacy and registration with the MP Pharmacy Council is required. Pay Scale: ₹25,300 – ₹80,500 (Level-6).
Ophthalmic Assistant
  • 10+2 (Intermediate) with PCB subjects and a Diploma in Ophthalmic Assistant is required, along with registration under the MP Paramedical Council. Pay Scale: ₹28,700 – ₹91,300 (Level-7).
O.T. Technician
  • 10+2 (Intermediate) with PCB subjects and a 1-year course in Operation Theatre Technician is required, along with registration under the MP Paramedical Council. Pay Scale: ₹25,300 – ₹80,500 (Level-6).
