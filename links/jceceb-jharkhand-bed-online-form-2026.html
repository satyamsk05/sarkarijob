Post Name Eligibility Criteria
JCECEB Jharkhand BEd CET
  • Candidates must have secured at least 50% marks (45% for SC/ST/OBC/PwD and other reserved categories) in their Bachelor’s and/or Master’s Degree in Science, Social Science, Humanities, or Commerce, or 55% marks (50% for reserved categories) in Bachelor’s in Engineering/Technology with specialization in Science and Mathematics. For M.Ed., a B.Ed. degree is mandatory. Candidates must choose one teaching subject studied in their qualifying degree with at least 50% marks out of 200 marks. There is no restriction on passing year, and final year students are also eligible, subject to fulfilling the required percentage at the time of admission.
