 How To Check & Download CCRAS Group A B C Final Answer Key 2026 
  • First, visit the official website of CCRAS (Central Council for Research in Ayurvedic Sciences).
  • On the homepage, go to the “Latest Announcements / What’s New” section.
  • Look for the notification titled “CCRAS Group A, B & C Final Answer Key 2026.”
  • Click on the Final Answer Key Download link provided with or below the notification.
  • A Login Page will appear on the screen.
  • To log in, enter your:
  • Registration Number / Application Number
  • Date of Birth (DOB)
  • Enter the captcha code and click on Login.
  • Your Final Answer Key will appear on the screen.
  • Finally, download the final answer key or take a printout for the exam.
