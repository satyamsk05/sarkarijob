How to Check Bihar Board 10th Result
  • Visit the official portal at results.biharboardonline.com.
  • Find and click the link for 'Matric Annual Examination Result 2026'.
  • Enter your valid Roll Code (5 Digits).
  • Enter your valid Roll Number (8 Digits).
  • Solve the simple Captcha (Arithmetic sum).
  • Click on 'View' to see your mark sheet.
