 How To Check & Download SBI Bank Clerk Mains Result 2026 
  • Visit the official website of the State Bank of India (SBI).
  • On the homepage, go to the Careers / Results section.
  • Look for the link that says “SBI Clerk Mains Result 2026.”
  • Click on the Result link when it becomes active after the announcement.
  • You may be asked to log in using your Registration Number / Roll Number and Date of Birth / Password (as required).
  • After logging in, your Clerk Mains Result / Scorecard will be displayed on the screen.
  • Download the result PDF or scorecard and save it to your device.
  • Print a clear copy of the result for future reference.
