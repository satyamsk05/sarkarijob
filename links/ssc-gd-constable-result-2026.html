SSC GD Constable Final Result & Score Card 2026

SSC GD in BSF, CISF, ITBP, CRPF, NCB, SSF, Assam Rifles

Final Result: 15 January 2026 Marks Released: 16 February 2026
Result Overview

Staff Selection Commission (SSC) has declared the Final Result for the Constable (GD) in Central Armed Police Forces (CAPFs), SSF, and Rifleman (GD) in Assam Rifles. Candidates who appeared for the Detailed Medical Examination (DME) and Document Verification (DV) can now check their final selection status. The Score Card / Individual Marks have also been released on 16/02/2026.

Steps to Check Score Card
  • Go to the 'Login' section on the SSC homepage (ssc.gov.in).
  • Enter your **Registration Number** and **Password**.
  • Go to the 'Result/Marks' tab in your dashboard.
  • Select the 'Constable (GD) in CAPFs, SSF and Rifleman (GD) Exam 2024'.
  • Click 'Submit' to view your detailed section-wise score.
