 How To Check & Download UPSC Civil Services IAS Reserve List 2026 
  • Visit the official website of the Union Public Service Commission — upsc.gov.in.
  • On the homepage, go to the “What’s New” or “Examination” section.
  • Find and click on the link titled “UPSC Civil Services (IAS) Reserve List 2026”.
  • The Reserve List PDF will open on your screen.
  • Check your Roll Number or Name in the list.
  • Download and print the PDF for future reference.
