 How To Check & Download MPPSC SES Admit Card 2026 
  • Open your internet browser on your mobile phone or computer.
  • Visit the official website of the Madhya Pradesh Public Service Commission.
  • On the homepage, go to the “Admit Card / Download Section”.
  • Look for the link titled “MPPSC State Engineering Services (SES) Admit Card 2026”.
  • Click on the admit card download link.
  • Enter your Application Number / Registration Number and Date of Birth.
  • Fill in the captcha code if required.
  • Click on the Submit / Login button.
  • Your MPPSC SES Admit Card 2026 will appear on the screen.
  • Download the admit card and take a printout for the examination.
