 How To Check & Download NTA CUET PG Admit Card 2026 
  • Visit the official website of the National Testing Agency (NTA).
  • On the homepage, go to the section for CUET PG (Common University Entrance Test – Postgraduate).
  • Look for the notification or link titled “CUET PG 2026 Exam City Details” or “Exam City Intimation”.
  • Click on the Exam City Details link when it is active.
  • Enter your Application Number / Form Number.
  • Enter your Date of Birth or Password (as required).
  • Fill in the security captcha/code if prompted.
  • Click the Submit / Login button.
  • Your Exam City Details will be displayed on the screen showing your assigned exam city for CUET PG 2026.
  • Download and save the PDF or details for future reference.
  • You may also print a copy to keep with your exam records.

 NTA CUET PG Admit Card 2026 : Important Question 
  • Question: Who conducts the CUET PG 2026 exam?

  • Answer: CUET PG (Common University Entrance Test – Postgraduate) is a national-level entrance exam for admission into PG courses in central, state, deemed, and participating universities.
