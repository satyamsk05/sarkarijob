 How to Enrolled With New Aadhar 
  • Find Your Nearest Enrollment Center to Choose Your State Name, District Name and Area.
  • Visit the center to submit your documents, along with a photograph and biometric details.
  • After a few days, your e-Aadhaar will be available for download from the official portal.
 
 Online Correction E Aadhar Card 
  • After the Successfully Generating the Aadhar Card if you See Any Correction in Aadhar Card.
  • For Any Correction Kindly Use Only Aadhar Card Correction Online or Enrollment Center.
  • After the Over the Correction Procedure Wait for the Few Days and Download E Aadhar.
 
 Document Required For New Aadhar Card Enrollment 
Photo ID Card
  • Passport, PAN Card, Ration/ PDS Photo Card, Voter ID, Driving License Etc.
Address Proof
  • Passport, Bank Statement/ Passbook, Post Office Account Statement/ Passbook , Ration Card ,Voter ID ,Driving License, Government Photo ID cards/ service photo identity card issued by PSU . Electricity Bill (not older than 3 months) , Water Bill (not older than 3 months) . Telephone Landline Bill (not older than 3 months), Property Tax Receipt (not older than 1 year) Etc.
Date of Birth
  • Birth Certificate, SSLC Book / Certificate, Passport, Photo ID card having Date of Birth, PAN Card, Marksheet, Etc.
 
 How to Download E Aadhar Card 
  • In order to print, verify or correct the Aadhar card, the applicant should open the official website of UIDAI.
  • Here the candidates will have to click on the useful link as per their requirements.
  • Applicants can get the electronic version of Aadhar Card by entering the Enrolment ID received at the time of enrolment in the registered mobile number.
  • Applicants who have enrolled for Aadhar Card can check their Aadhar generation status by providing the enrolment ID and their Date of Birth.
  • In order to get Online /Offline Aadhar form (Hindi/English) and Update /Correction Form (Online/Offline) applicants shall be required to open the useful links as per their requirements.
