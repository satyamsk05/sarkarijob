NTA NEET UG Admission 2026-27 Notification

Exam Date: 03 May 2026 Status: Correction Window Closed
Entrance Overview

The National Testing Agency (NTA) is conducting the NEET (UG) - 2026 for admission to undergraduate medical courses (MBBS/BDS/BAMS/BSMS/BUMS/BHMS) in all medical institutions. The exam will be held on 03 May 2026 (Sunday). Candidates who have already applied can check the official portal for exam center and admit card updates.
