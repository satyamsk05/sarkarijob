 How To Check & Download RRB Section Controller Answer Key 2026 
  • Visit the official website of the Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) for the region where you submitted your application.
  • On the homepage, go to the Notifications / Candidate Updates / Answer Key section.
  • Look for the notification that says “RRB Section Controller Answer Key 2026.”
  • Click on the Answer Key link once it is active.
  • You may be asked to log in using your Registration Number / Roll Number and Date of Birth (or password).
  • After logging in, your provisional answer key and response sheet will be displayed on the screen.
  • Download the answer key PDF and save it to your device.
  • You can use the answer key to estimate your expected score before the final result is out.
