 How To Check & Download SSC MTS / Havaldar Answer Key 2026 
  • Open an internet browser on your phone or computer.
  • Go to the official website of the Staff Selection Commission (SSC), or the regional SSC website relevant to your exam city.
  • On the homepage, find the Admit Card / Candidate Login section.
  • Look for the notification titled SSC MTS / Havaldar Answer Key 2026.
  • Click on that admit card link to open the login page.
  • Enter your Registration Number / Roll Number and Date of Birth / Password in the required fields.
  • Submit the details to view your Answer Key on the screen.
  • Carefully check all details shown — your name, roll number, exam date & shift, exam centre address, reporting time, photo, and signature.
  • Download the Answer Key PDF and save it on your device.
  • Take a clear printout of the exam city — you must carry this printout to the exam centre.
