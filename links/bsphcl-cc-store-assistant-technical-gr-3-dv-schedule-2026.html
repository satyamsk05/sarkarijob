 How To Check & Download BSPHCL CC/ Store Assistant & Technical Gr-3 DV Schedule 2026 
  • Open your internet browser on your mobile phone or computer.
  • Go to the official website of the Bihar State Power Holding Company Limited.
  • On the homepage, find the “Recruitment / Career” section.
  • Look for the notification titled “CC, Store Assistant & Technical Grade-3 DV Schedule 2026”.
  • Click on the document verification schedule link.
  • The official DV schedule PDF will open on the screen.
  • Check the document verification date, reporting time, and venue details mentioned in the notice.
  • Press Ctrl + F and enter your Roll Number or Registration Number to find your details.
  • Download the DV schedule PDF for reference.
  • Take a printout of the schedule for use during the document verification process.
