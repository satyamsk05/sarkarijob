 How To Check & Download MP Police ASI and HC Admit Card 2026 
  • Open your internet browser on your mobile phone or computer.
  • Visit the official website of the Madhya Pradesh Professional Examination Board.
  • On the homepage, go to the “Admit Card” section.
  • Look for the link titled “MP Police ASI & HC Admit Card 2026”.
  • Click on the admit card download link.
  • Enter your Application Number / Registration Number and Date of Birth.
