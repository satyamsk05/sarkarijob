 MP RTE Online Form 2026 : Eligibility & Required Documents 
Eligibility Criteria Documents Required
Disadvantaged Group
  • Caste Certificate issued by the Tehsildar
Residence Proof
  • Residence Certificate issued at Tehsil level, Voter ID Card, Ration Card, Rural Area Job Card, Passport, Driving License, Electricity Bill, and Water Bill
Weaker Section (EWS)
  • Income Certificate (annual) issued by the Tehsil level or card issued for Below Poverty Line (Antyodaya) by the Food & Civil Supplies Department
Child of Parent/Guardian suffering from AIDS, HIV, or Cancer
  • Medical Certificate issued by the Chief Medical Officer
Orphan Child
  • Certificate issued by the Competent Authority
Child of Disabled / Elderly / Widow Pension Beneficiary Parent or Guardian
  • Certificate issued by the Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities / Health Department / Social Welfare Department
