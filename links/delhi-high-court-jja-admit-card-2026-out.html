 How To Check & Download Delhi High Court JJA Admit Card 2026 
  • Open your internet browser on your mobile phone or computer.
  • Visit the official website of the Delhi High Court.
  • On the homepage, go to the “Public Notices / Recruitment” section.
  • Look for the link titled “Delhi High Court JJA Admit Card 2026”.
  • Click on the admit card download link.
  • Enter your Registration Number / Application Number and Date of Birth.
  • Fill in the captcha code if required.
  • Click on the Submit / Login button.
  • Your JJA Admit Card will appear on the screen.
  • Download the admit card and take a printout for the examination.
