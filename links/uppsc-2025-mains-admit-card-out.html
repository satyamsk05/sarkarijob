 How To Check & Download UPPSC PCS Mains Admit Card 2026 
  • Open your internet browser on your mobile phone or computer.
  • Go to the official website of the Uttar Pradesh Public Service Commission.
  • On the homepage, look for the “Latest Updates” or “Examination / Notices” section.
  • Find the notification titled “UPPSC PCS Mains Admit Card 2026”.
  • Click on the exam date notice link.
  • The official exam schedule PDF will open on the screen.
  • Check the Mains exam date, exam timing, and instructions mentioned in the notice.
  • Click on the Download option to save the PDF file.
  • Take a printout of the exam schedule for future reference.
