 How To Check & Download UP Police Recruitment Calendar 2025-26 
  • Visit the Official Website
  • Go to the Uttar Pradesh Police Recruitment & Promotion Board (UPPRPB) official portal.
  • Find the Recruitment Calendar Link
  • On the homepage, look for the notification “UP Police Recruitment Calendar 2025–26” under the latest updates/notice section.
  • Open the Calendar PDF
  • Click on the link, and the Recruitment Calendar PDF will open showing post-wise vacancy details, notification dates, and exam schedules.
  • Download the Calendar
  • Click on the Download option to save the PDF on your device.
  • Take a Printout (Optional)
  • Keep a hard copy of the calendar for quick reference during your exam preparation.
 
 UP Police Upcoming Exam Information 2025 : Application Process 
  • Online Registration
  • Visit the UPPRPB official website.
  • Click on the link for the desired UP Police recruitment post (Constable, SI, Jail Warder, Computer Operator, etc.).
  • Register with basic details like name, mobile number, and email ID.
  • Filling the Application Form
  • Log in with your registration credentials.
  • Fill in personal details, educational qualifications, and category information carefully.
  • Select your exam center preference (if option available).
  • Uploading Documents
  • Upload scanned copies of your passport-size photograph, signature, and other required documents in the prescribed format.
  • Payment of Application Fee
  • Pay the required application fee online using Debit Card, Credit Card, Net Banking, or UPI.
  • Final Submission
  • Review the form carefully before final submission.
  • Click on Submit and take a printout of the application form and fee receipt for future reference.
