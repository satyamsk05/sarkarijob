Post Name Eligibility Criteria
Assistant Teacher (Male-Female) Hindi
  • Candidates must have a graduation degree with Hindi as a subject and must have studied Sanskrit at the intermediate level or passed the Uttar Madhyama exam from UP Sanskrit Shiksha Parishad. They must also have a B.Ed. degree from an institute recognized by NCTE in India.
Assistant Teacher (Male-Female) English
  • Candidates must have a graduation degree in English Literature from a recognized university and a B.Ed. degree from an institute approved by NCTE in India.
Assistant Teacher (Male-Female) Maths
  • Candidates must have a graduation degree with Mathematics as a subject from a recognized university and a B.Ed. degree from an institute approved by NCTE in India.
Assistant Teacher (Male-Female) Science
  • Candidates must have a graduation degree with Physics and Chemistry as subjects from a recognized university and a B.Ed. degree from an institute recognized by NCTE in India.
Assistant Teacher (Male-Female) Social Science
  • Candidates must have a graduation degree with at least two subjects from History, Geography, Political Science, and Economics from a recognized university, along with a B.Ed. degree from an institute approved by NCTE in India.
Assistant Teacher (Male-Female) Computer
  • Candidates must have a B.Tech/B.E. in Computer Science, OR a graduate degree in Computer Science or Computer Applications, OR a graduation degree with 'A' Level certification from NIELIT, OR B.Tech in Computer Science and MCA. A B.Ed. degree recognized by NCTE is a preferential qualification.
Assistant Teacher (Male-Female) Urdu
  • Candidates must have a graduation degree with Urdu as a subject from a recognized university and a B.Ed. degree from an institute approved by NCTE in India.
Assistant Teacher (Male-Female) Biology
  • Candidates must have a graduation degree with Zoology and Botany as subjects from a recognized university and a B.Ed. degree from an institute approved by NCTE in India.
Assistant Teacher (Male-Female) Sanskrit
  • Candidates must have a graduation degree with Sanskrit as a subject from a recognized university and a B.Ed. degree from an institute approved by NCTE in India.
Assistant Teacher (Male-Female) Arts
  • Candidates must have a graduation degree with Art as a subject along with a B.Ed. degree recognized by NCTE, or a Bachelor’s degree in Fine Arts (BFA) with a B.Ed. degree from a recognized university, where B.Ed. is considered a preferential qualification.
Assistant Teacher (Male-Female) Music
  • Candidates must have a graduation degree with Music as a subject from a recognized university, or have a Sangeet Visharad from Bhatkhande Sangeet Mahavidyalaya or Sangeet Prabhakar from Prayag Sangeet Samiti, Allahabad along with a graduation degree from a recognized university. Additionally, they must have a B.Ed. degree recognized by NCTE in India.
Assistant Teacher (Male-Female) Commerce
  • Candidates must have a graduation degree in Commerce from a recognized university and a B.Ed. degree from an institute approved by NCTE in India.
Assistant Teacher (Male-Female) Physical Education
  • Candidates must have a graduation degree from a recognized university and a B.P.Ed. or B.P.E. degree in a course approved by NCTE in India.
Assistant Teacher (Male-Female) Home Science
  • Candidates must have a graduation degree with Home Science as a subject from a recognized university and a B.Ed. degree from an institute approved by NCTE in India.
Assistant Teacher (Male) Agriculture / Horticulture
  • Candidates must have a graduation degree in Agriculture or Horticulture from a recognized university and a B.Ed. degree from an institute approved by NCTE in India.

 How To Check & Download UPPSC LT Grade Assistant Teacher Result 2026 
  • Open the official website of Uttar Pradesh Public Service Commission (UPPSC) in your web browser.
  • On the homepage, go to the Results / Notifications section.

  • Download the result PDF and save it for future reference.

 UPPSC LT Grade Assistant Teacher Result 2026 : Important Question 
  • Q. When will the UPPSC LT Grade Result 2026 be released?
  • A. The admit card is usually released a few weeks before the exam date. Candidates should regularly check the official UPPSC website.
  • Q. How can I download my answer key?
  • A. Visit the UPPSC official website, navigate to the answer key / Download section, enter your Registration Number / Roll Number and Date of Birth / Password, then download and print the admit card.
  • Q. What details are mentioned on the admit card?
  • A. Candidate’s name, roll number, photograph, signature, exam date, time, exam center, and important instructions.
  • Q. Is it mandatory to carry the admit card to the exam?
  • A. Yes, without the admit card, entry to the exam hall is not allowed. Always carry a valid photo ID along with it.
  • Q. What should I do if there is an error in my admit card?
  • A. Contact UPPSC helpdesk immediately or use the correction facility if announced. Errors in name, date of birth, or category should be corrected before the exam.
