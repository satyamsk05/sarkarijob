 How To Check & Download BTSC X-Ray Technician DV List 2026 
  • Open your internet browser on your mobile phone or computer.
  • Visit the official website of the Bihar Technical Service Commission.
  • On the homepage, go to the “Results / Notices” section.
  • Look for the link titled “BTSC X-Ray Technician DV List 2026”.
  • Click on the DV list link.
  • The DV List PDF will open on the screen.
  • Press Ctrl + F and enter your Roll Number or Name to search.
  • If your name or roll number appears, you are selected for Document Verification (DV).
  • Download the DV List PDF for future use.
  • Take a printout for reference during the DV process.
