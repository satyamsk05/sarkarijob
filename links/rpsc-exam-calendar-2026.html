 How To Check & Download RPSC Exam Calendar 2026 
  • Visit the official website of Rajasthan Public Service Commission (RPSC).
  • On the homepage, go to the “Examination Schedule / Exam Calendar / Notifications” section.
  • Look for the notification titled RPSC Exam Calendar 2026.
  • Click on the Exam Calendar notification.
  • The exam calendar will open in PDF format or as a notice.
  • Carefully check the dates of all exams, application periods, and important timelines mentioned in the calendar.
  • Download and save the exam calendar on your device.
  • Take a printout if you want a physical copy for planning.
