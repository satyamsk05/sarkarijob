UPPSC Assistant Registrar Interview Letter 2026

UP University Central Service (Assistant Registrar) Recruitment 2024

Link Date: 13 March 2026 Advt No: A5/E-1/2024
Interview Update

Uttar Pradesh Public Service Commission (UPPSC) has released the Interview Letter for the Assistant Registrar Exam 2024. Candidates who qualified in the written examination can now download their interview call letters. Interviews will be held on 23rd and 24th March 2026.

Important Dates

Written Exam Date27-28 April 2025
Written Result Date05/02/2026
Interview Letter Released13/03/2026
Interview Dates23-24 March 2026

Post Details

Total Vacancies38 Posts
OrganizationUPPSC
Service NameUP Univ Central Service
Steps to Download Call Letter
  • Visit the official UPPSC website (uppsc.up.nic.in).
  • Click on the link 'Interview Letter for Assistant Registrar Exam 2024'.
  • Enter your OTR Number or Registration Number.
  • Verify your Date of Birth and Gender.
  • Submit to download and print your Interview Call Letter.
