The Board of Revenue Departments of Bihar provide online services for land record verification. These services include checking and downloading certificates for Land Records, Khasra, Khatauni, Land Maps, and Bhu Naksha. All individuals interested in viewing land details in Bihar can find complete information regarding Dakhil Kharij, registration, and more below.  How To Check & Verify Khasara, Khataun, Bhulekh 
  • To check or verify the online certificate, candidates must visit the important link provided in the section below.
  • Once on the page, click on the verification link either Server I or Old Server II.
  • Next, select the district where the land is located (e.g., Agra, Allahabad, Firozabad, Kanpur, Varanasi, etc.).
  • Then, choose or enter the Tehsil Name in the designated field.
  • After that, enter the Village/Area Name in the given box.
  • Finally, input your Khasara Number in the appropriate field.
  • You will then be able to view and verify the land record and related details.
